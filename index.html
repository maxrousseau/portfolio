About me

My name is Maxime Rousseau, owner of Rousseau Orthodontistes.

I completed my orthodontics specialty and MSc at the University of Montreal in 2024. I graduated as a DMD from McGill University in 2020 followed by a general practice residency at the Montreal Children's Hospital.
